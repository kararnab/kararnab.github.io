Communications App Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 18, 2024

Data Collection

Communications does not currently collect any personal data or information from its users.

Permissions

This app currently works with NO PERMISSION (Not even Internet) but may require certain permissions to function properly in future, such as access to your device's Internet and camera for making backups, and saving images of your contacts for facilitating communication. These permissions are used solely for the intended purpose of the app and are not stored or transmitted to any third parties.

Future Features

While the app does not currently have data backup and recovery features, we may consider adding them in the future. Any future features that involve the collection or storage of user data will be designed with privacy in mind. Specifically, these features will be end-to-end encrypted, ensuring that your data remains private and secure.

Use of Contact Details

Communications does not share any contact details entered by users within the app with any third parties. Your contact information is solely used within the app for its intended purpose and is not shared, sold, or distributed to any external parties.

Age Restrictions

Communications is a utility app designed for general use, and it is suitable for individuals of all ages. Since the app does not have Internet access and does not collect any personal information, there are no age restrictions for its use. Users of all ages can safely use the app without concern about their personal information being collected or shared.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates. Your continued use of the Communications app after any changes to this Privacy Policy will signify your acceptance of those changes.
