Communications App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 19, 2025

Data Collection

Communications does not collect any personal data from its users. All information entered into the app is stored locally on the user’s device and is not transmitted to any servers or shared with third parties.

Permissions

Currently, the app does not require any special permissions, including Internet access. If permissions are introduced in the future, they will be clearly disclosed and used only for their stated purpose, and only after user consent.

Future Features

While the app does not currently include data backup or recovery features, such functionality may be added in the future. Any future features involving the storage or processing of user data will be designed with privacy in mind and protected using end-to-end encryption where applicable.

Use of Contact Details

Contact details entered by users are used solely within the app for its intended functionality. This information remains on the user’s device and is never shared, sold, or transmitted to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Communications does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or external libraries that collect or transmit user data.

Age Restrictions

Communications is suitable for users of all ages. The app does not collect, transmit, or share personal data. Parents and guardians are encouraged to supervise usage by minors where appropriate.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, regulatory, or operational reasons. Users are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the app after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
